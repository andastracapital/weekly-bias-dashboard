# Prop-Firm Mandatory Close Events Table

Source: Official prop-firm rules document (TK Trading Fundamentals)

## Per Currency

| Currency | Affected Instruments | Mandatory Close Events |
|----------|---------------------|------------------------|
| **USD** | Forex + Gold + US Indices + DXY | Federal Funds Rate & Statement, Non-Farm Employment Change, Unemployment Rate & Wages, Advance GDP q/q, FOMC Meeting Minutes, CPI y/y |
| **EUR** | Forex only | Main Refinancing Rate (ECB Rate Decision) |
| **GBP** | Forex only | Official Bank Rate & MPC Votes, CPI y/y |
| **CAD** | Forex only | Overnight Rate / BOC Rate Statement, CPI m/m, Employment Change / Unemployment Rate |
| **AUD** | Forex only | Cash Rate & RBA Statement, Employment Change / Unemployment Rate, CPI q/q, GDP q/q |
| **NZD** | Forex only | Official Cash Rate & RBNZ Rate Statement, Employment Change / Unemployment Rate, CPI q/q, GDP q/q |
| **CHF** | Forex only | SNB Policy Rate, SNB Monetary Policy Statement, SNB Press Conference |

## Important Exclusions

| Event | Reason |
|-------|--------|
| ADP Non-Farm Employment Change | **Private report** (ADP company), NOT the official BLS NFP. Released Wednesday, not Friday. Must be excluded. |
| ISM Manufacturing PMI | High impact but NOT a prop-firm mandatory close event |
| ISM Services PMI | High impact but NOT a prop-firm mandatory close event |
| Retail Sales | High impact but NOT a prop-firm mandatory close event |

## Timing Notes

- **USD NFP** always released on the **first Friday** of the month at 14:30 Frankfurt Time
- **Rate Decisions** (Fed, ECB, BoE, BoC, RBA, RBNZ, SNB) vary by meeting schedule
- **AUD/NZD events** often occur before 09:00 Frankfurt Time (overnight for European traders)
- **JPY/BoJ events** often occur between 02:00-05:00 Frankfurt Time (overnight)

## Overnight Events (Before 09:00 Frankfurt Time)

These events require **early warning from 16:00 the previous day**:

| Currency | Typical Time (Frankfurt) | Event |
|----------|--------------------------|-------|
| AUD | 01:30 - 05:30 | RBA Rate Decision, GDP q/q, Employment Change |
| NZD | 02:00 - 04:00 | RBNZ Rate Decision, GDP q/q |
| JPY | 02:00 - 05:00 | BoJ Rate Decision, Monetary Policy Statement |
