# Design Brainstorming: Weekly Bias FX Dashboard

<text>
<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Institutional Brutalism / Bloomberg Terminal Chic**
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Data Density**: Information is dense but strictly organized; no wasted space.
  2. **High Contrast**: Dark backgrounds (almost black) with neon accents (green, red, amber) for immediate readability.
  3. **Raw Functionality**: Exposed grids, monospaced fonts for data, sharp edges, no rounded corners.
  4. **Speed**: Visuals imply real-time updates and professional precision.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: Deep Obsidian (#050505) - minimizes eye strain for long sessions.
  - **Accents**: Terminal Green (#00FF41) for Bullish, Alert Red (#FF3333) for Bearish, Amber (#FFC000) for Neutral/Warning.
  - **Text**: Off-white (#E0E0E0) for primary, dimmed grey (#808080) for secondary.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Modular Grid**: A strict bento-box style grid where every component is a self-contained module with visible borders.
  - **Sidebar Navigation**: Fixed left sidebar for quick access to different views (Dashboard, Calendar, Settings).
  - **Ticker Tape**: A scrolling ticker at the top for critical headlines.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Monospaced Data**: All numerical data and tickers in a high-quality monospaced font (e.g., JetBrains Mono or Roboto Mono).
  - **Scanlines/Grid Overlays**: Subtle background grid patterns to reinforce the "terminal" feel.
  - **Status Indicators**: Blinking dots or small bars to indicate "Live" or "Updated".

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Instant Feedback**: Hover states are sharp and immediate (e.g., border color change).
  - **Keyboard First**: Design implies efficiency; hotkeys for filtering (e.g., Press 'B' for Bullish).

  **Animation**:
  - **Glitch/Typewriter**: Text loads with a quick typewriter effect.
  - **Flash Updates**: Data changes flash briefly to draw attention.
  - **No Smooth Easing**: Transitions are linear or stepped, avoiding "soft" feels.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Chakra Petch** or **Rajdhani** (Tech/Futuristic sans).
  - **Body/Data**: **JetBrains Mono** or **Fira Code**.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Swiss International Style / Modern Financial Editorial**
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Clarity & Hierarchy**: Content is king; typography dictates importance.
  2. **Asymmetry**: Dynamic layouts that break the grid to create visual interest.
  3. **Cleanliness**: ample whitespace, lack of ornamentation, focus on the essential.
  4. **Trust**: A polished, "magazine-like" aesthetic that conveys authority and reliability.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: Crisp White (#FFFFFF) or very light grey (#F5F5F7).
  - **Accents**: Deep Navy (#0A2540) for structure, Swiss Red (#FF3B30) for emphasis/bearish, Emerald (#34C759) for bullish.
  - **Text**: Stark Black (#000000) for high contrast reading.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Columnar**: Strong vertical rhythm with clear columns for text and data.
  - **Big Type**: Large, bold headings to guide the eye.
  - **Card-based**: Information grouped in clean cards with subtle shadows.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Grotesque Typography**: Heavy use of Helvetica Now or Inter in various weights.
  - **Rule Lines**: Thin horizontal lines to separate sections (editorial style).
  - **Abstract Data Viz**: Clean, minimal charts without clutter.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Smooth & Deliberate**: Transitions are soft and fluid.
  - **Readability Focused**: Hover effects increase contrast or slightly lift cards.

  **Animation**:
  - **Fade & Slide**: Elements slide gently into place.
  - **Smooth Scaling**: Charts grow smoothly.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Inter** (Tight tracking, heavy weights) or **Helvetica Now Display**.
  - **Body**: **Inter** or **Roboto** (highly readable).
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Glassmorphism / Neo-Futurism**
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Translucency**: Frosted glass effects to create depth and context.
  2. **Vibrancy**: Vivid, glowing gradients to represent energy and market movement.
  3. **Softness**: Rounded corners, blurred backgrounds, floating elements.
  4. **Immersive**: The UI feels like a heads-up display (HUD) floating in space.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: Dark, deep gradient (Midnight Blue to Purple).
  - **Accents**: Neon Cyan, Hot Pink, Electric Blue for data points.
  - **Glass**: White with low opacity and background blur.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Floating Cards**: Content lives on translucent layers floating above the background.
  - **Central Focus**: Key data (Weekly Bias) is central and glowing.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Backdrop Filter**: Heavy use of `backdrop-filter: blur()`.
  - **Gradients**: Mesh gradients as backgrounds.
  - **Glowing Borders**: Subtle inner shadows and borders to define edges.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Tactile**: Cards feel like physical glass panes.
  - **Parallax**: Subtle movement of background layers on mouse move.

  **Animation**:
  - **Float**: Elements gently bob or float.
  - **Glow Pulse**: Critical alerts pulse softly.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Outfit** or **Space Grotesk** (Modern, geometric).
  - **Body**: **Quicksand** or **Nunito** (Rounded, friendly).
</idea>
</text>
